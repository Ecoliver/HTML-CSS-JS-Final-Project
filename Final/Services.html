My Services - Facials

No Man Left Behind Facial

This facial is tailored to meet the specific skincare needs of men. This facial addresses concerns such as razor burn, ingrown hairs, and skins sensitivity caused by shaving. The treatment typically includes deep cleaning, exfoliation, and moisturizing, helping to soothe and hydrate the skin.

Glow With The Flow Facial

This is our customizable facial which is tailored to your skins specific needs. I will assess your skin which will then allow me to customize a treatment, suitable to your skin, using specific products and techniques. This facial ensures that your skin receives personalized care made specifically for you.

Not a Plane Jane Facial

This facial is a non-invasive exfoliation procedure that removes dead skin cells and fine vellus hair (peach fuzz) from the surface of your skin. This treatment can improve the skin's texture, making it smoother and radiant.

Zit Happens Facial

This facial is specifically designed for acne-prone skin and helps target issues such as excess oil, clogged pores, and inflammation. This facial includes a deep cleansing exfoliation, extractions, and specialized masks or treatments to reduce acne and promote clearer skin. This facial is tailored to address acne concerns without causing furter irritation.

I'm Peeling Myself Facial

This facial is a cosmetic treatment that uses chemical solutions to remove the damaged outer layers of the skin. This process encourages skin regeneration and can improve various skin concerns such as fine lines, wrinkles, acne scars, and uneven skin tone. This facial includes a cleansing, a chemical peel, a mask to reduce any redness due to the peel, and moisturizer/SPF.
